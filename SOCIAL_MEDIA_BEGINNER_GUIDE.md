### DAY 3-4: Learn the Basics (What Makes Videos Go Viral)

#### The 3-Second Rule:
**If you don't hook them in 3 seconds, they scroll.**

**BAD hook**: "Hey guys, welcome back to my channel..."
**GOOD hook**: "You call yourself a Christian but can't quote a single verse when your friend is suicidal."

**Why it works**: Shock + truth + challenge = they keep watching.

---

#### Video Structure (for 30-60 sec videos):
```
Seconds 0-3:   HOOK (shocking statement, question, or bold claim)
Seconds 3-15:  PROBLEM (paint the pain, make them feel it)
Seconds 15-40: SOLUTION (your app, your challenge, what you're doing)
Seconds 40-60: CTA (call to action - "Join me, link in bio")
```

**Example**:
- 0-3: "87% of Christians can't quote Scripture when they need it most."
- 3-15: "You're fighting anxiety, fear, temptation every day‚ÄîUNARMED."
- 15-40: "I'm changing that. 30 verses in 30 days. Proving memorization works."
- 40-60: "Join me. Free. Link in bio. We start Dec 1."

---

#### Best Filming Setup (FREE):

**Location**: Film in the same spot every day (consistency)
- Near a window (natural light)
- Clean background (not messy room)
- Quiet (no TV, dogs barking, etc.)

**Lighting**:
- Film during daytime (10am-3pm = best natural light)
- Face the window (light on your face, not behind you)
- If nighttime: Turn on all lights in room + one lamp pointed at ceiling (bounces light)

**Camera Position**:
- Phone at EYE LEVEL (not looking up at you, not looking down)
- Arm's length away (not too close, not too far)
- VERTICAL (9:16 ratio - TikTok/Instagram format)

**Audio**:
- Speak LOUD and CLEAR
- Get close to phone (2-3 feet max)
- Turn off AC/fans (background noise kills videos)

## üì± WEEK 2: PRACTICE (Nov 23-30)

### DAY 8-10: Download CapCut & Learn Editing (1 hour total)

#### What is CapCut?
FREE video editing app (owned by TikTok). Easiest editor for beginners.

**Download**:
- iPhone: App Store ‚Üí Search "CapCut"
- Android: Play Store ‚Üí Search "CapCut"

---

#### CapCut Basics (10-minute tutorial):

**Step 1**: Import video
- Open CapCut ‚Üí New Project
- Select video from your phone
- Tap "Add"

**Step 2**: Trim video (remove bad parts)
- Drag white bars at start/end to trim
- Cut out mistakes: Tap video ‚Üí Split ‚Üí Delete bad section

**Step 3**: Add captions (AUTO CAPTIONS - game changer):
- Tap "Text" at bottom
- Tap "Auto Captions"
- CapCut transcribes your words automatically!
- Edit any mistakes
- Change font/color if you want (optional)

**Step 4**: Add music (optional):
- Tap "Audio" ‚Üí "Sounds"
- Search "motivational" or "inspiring"
- Lower volume to 20% (your voice should be louder)

**Step 5**: Export
- Tap "Export" (top right)
- Choose "1080p" quality
- Wait for it to process
- Save to your phone

**DONE. That's it.** Seriously, 90% of viral TikToks use these 5 steps.

## üí° PRO TIPS (Steal These)

### Tip 1: Show Your Face
People connect with FACES, not logos. Every video should show YOU talking to camera.

### Tip 2: First Draft = Posted Draft
Don't over-edit. Scrappy, authentic videos outperform polished ones. Film it, caption it, post it. Move on.

### Tip 3: Reply to Every Comment (First 24 Hours)
TikTok's algorithm rewards engagement. If you reply to comments, TikTok shows your video to more people.

### Tip 4: Steal What Works
Watch videos in #BibleMemory and #ChristianTikTok. If a video has 100K+ views, study it:
- What was the hook?
- How long was the video?
- What did they say in caption?
- Copy the structure (not the words)

### Tip 5: Post at the SAME TIME Every Day
Consistency trains your audience. If you post at 11am daily, they'll check TikTok at 11am looking for you.

### Tip 6: Don't Check Views Obsessively
Your first 10 videos might get 100-500 views each. That's NORMAL. One video will pop (10K+ views), then the algorithm will push your others.

## ‚ùì FAQ: "But I'm Not Good at This..."

**Q: I'm awkward on camera. Help.**
A: Everyone is awkward at first. Film 10 practice videos. Delete them. By video 11, you'll be comfortable. Also: Awkward = relatable. Polished = boring.

**Q: What if nobody watches my videos?**
A: Your first 10 videos will get low views. Post anyway. Video #11 might explode. You never know which one the algorithm will love.

**Q: What if people leave mean comments?**
A: They will. Ignore trolls. Reply to genuine criticism. Delete/block hate speech. Thick skin required.

**Q: Do I need to show my face?**
A: YES. Faceless videos get 10x fewer views. People follow PEOPLE, not brands.

**Q: Can I hire someone to do this for me?**
A: No. The whole point is YOU doing the challenge. Outsourcing kills authenticity.

**Q: What if I mess up and forget a verse?**
A: PERFECT. Film it. "Day 15: I completely blanked on this verse. This is why we practice." = Viral content.
