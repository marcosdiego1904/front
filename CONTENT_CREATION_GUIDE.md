## ðŸŽ¬ PHASE B: TECHNICAL SETUP (Nov 22-24)

### iPhone 16 Video Settings

**Camera App Settings:**
- **Resolution**: 1080p at 60fps (best for TikTok/Instagram)
- **Format**: Most Compatible (not High Efficiency)
- **Grid**: ON (helps with rule of thirds composition)
- **Cinematic Mode**: OFF (use standard video unless doing creative effects)
- **Front vs Back Camera**: Front camera for authenticity (talking to viewers)

**Composition Tips:**
- Keep your face in upper 2/3 of frame (leave room for text at bottom)
- Eye level with camera (not looking down or up)
- Arm's length distance (not too close, not too far)

### Lighting Strategy

**Best Free Option: Natural Window Light**
- Film FACING the window (you're lit from front)
- Avoid having window BEHIND you (creates silhouette)
- Best times: 8-10am (soft morning light) or 4-6pm (golden hour)

**Cloudy Days**: Actually BETTER than harsh sun (soft, even lighting)

**Night/Indoor**:
- Turn on all room lights
- Film near lamps
- Consider: $15-25 ring light from Amazon (optional but helpful)

**Lighting Mistakes to Avoid:**
- Overhead lights only (creates shadows under eyes)
- Backlighting (window behind you)
- Mixed color temps (warm + cool lights together)

### Audio Quality

**iPhone 16 Built-in Mic**: Actually pretty good!

**Audio Tips:**
- Film in quiet room
- Turn off AC, fans, TV
- Avoid echo-y rooms (bathrooms, empty rooms)
- Speak clearly and project voice

**Optional Upgrade ($20-30):**
- Lavalier (lapel) mic that plugs into iPhone
- Clips to shirt, captures clear voice
- Brands: Rode, Boya, PowerDeWise
- **Game changer for quality**

### AI-Assisted Script Creation Workflow

**Daily Process (15 minutes):**

**Step 1: Prompt ChatGPT**
```
"I'm creating a [LENGTH]-second TikTok video.

ABOUT ME:
- 23-year-old founder
- Passionate but not preachy
- Joyful, energetic, relatable
- English is second language (keep language simple)

TOPIC: [Insert topic]

TARGET AUDIENCE: Christians 25-40 who struggle with Bible memory

TONE: Passionate, authentic, joyful, slightly controversial

OUTPUT:
- 3 hook options (first 3 seconds)
- 30-40 second body
- Clear CTA (call-to-action)

Keep sentences short. Use conversational language."
```

**Step 2: Customize to YOUR Voice**
- Read script out loud
- Change words that don't feel natural
- Add YOUR personality (jokes, expressions)
- Simplify any complex words

**Step 3: Practice (Don't Memorize)**
- Read script 3-5 times
- Internalize the IDEA, not exact words
- Practice with phone camera (don't film yet)

**Step 4: Film**
- Don't read from script
- Talk naturally (like explaining to a friend)
- Do 3-5 takes
- Pick the best one
